Depression Journey

Depression can be a long and arduous journey, but many have found a way to emerge stronger on the other side. These are the stories of individuals who have struggled with depression but found resilience within themselves to keep going. Through support, therapy, medication, or simply refusing to give up, these stories demonstrate the power of human spirit and determination.

Financial Crisis Was Hard On Me – Anonymous, 52

CS Team February 18th, 2020

I was stressed and under depression when I had no money to take care of my family, my business failed and even I had a heart attack. It happened all of a sudden (actually it was going for 4 years) but after the surgery, I understood that it was major depression that was drowning my physical health too. Later, I started practicing Yoga religiously and now I am in a better state. I want to tell you all to take care of your mental as well as physical health.
